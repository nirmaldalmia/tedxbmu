Emcee auditions

Date and time : 20 January, 2017, 2 PM onwards

The level of excitement is increasing at a alarming pace as the first ever TEDx BMU event of our university is less than a month away. We are happy to inform that we are accepting applications for EMCEE. The interested students are required to give the auditions for the same on February (20 Jan,2017) at 2:00 pm. The venue will be informed to you shortly.

In the message field, please let us know of your questions and other things you want to ask.

The EMCEE selection process:

1. Send in your application by the deadline on the website.

2. Please be on time in order to avoid inconvenience.

Incentives:

1. Exposure to address the esteemed audience through a big platform like TEDx.

2. The chance of being the first face to TEDx BMU EMCEE.

The EMCEE is expected to be confident, fluent in English and interactive body language.
