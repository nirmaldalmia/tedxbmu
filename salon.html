TEDxBMUSalon

25 November 2017

Broadening the horizon

In a nutshell, the theme of this particular event or rather TEDx Salon means thinking out of the box. The various speakers share their experiences which in turn helps the audience to connect it to their lives. These experiences help one expand their range of interests, activities and knowledge. Any solution that the speakers may have used in their lives could be used by the audience in their own lives too. This would help the audience expand their knowledge, discover new things, enlarge their understanding, familiarize themselves with a broader range of concepts and understand the human experience.

Sandhya Suri

Sandhya Suri is an ex-Naval Officer, proudly one of the first women officers to serve on warship, and a cross functional professional with both domestic and international experience spanning a period of over two decades. A hands on highly adaptive and creative person, she has the ability to positively approach challenges. Her diverse experience has enabled her to set up Human Resources and Administrative Operations for two key conglomerates in Nigeria.

She currently covers a multi-faced role as a Management Consultant, Motivational Speaker, and Victory Coach. The third Indian Naval Academy batch had got 14 lady cadets. One among them was, Sandhya Suri, who was preparing for her IPS exams back then when she received the joining instructions in 1994. As her fate might have it, she rose to become one from the handful of women officers to be posted on board a warship.

FAQ

Holla @BMU if you've got more questions and we'll do our best to answer.

  • What is TEDxBMUSalon?

    Salon events are often weekly or monthly events that keeps a TEDx community engaged between regular TEDx events. It's a unique kind of gathering that TEDx organizers hold which allows the conversation to continue, in person. To keep the community of TEDx alive and active, we've come up with the idea of organising TEDxBMUSalon, which'll feature some of the best speakers available in the region and try to create an environment to give the student community a platform for continuous development.

  • When and where will TEDxBMU take place this year?

    TEDxBMUSalon will take place on November 25, 2017 at BML Munjal University, Gurgaon, Haryana.

  • I would like to help organise this year's conference - how do I get involved?

    Unfortunately, our formal volunteer application process is open only to the students of the university. We appreciate your interest in and support of TEDxBMU.

  • Can anyone attend TEDxBMUSalon as an audience? Where can I buy a ticket?

    Yes! Everyone is invited and welcome to attend the event. However, tickets are limited as we have limitations from TED; so we have a filtering mechanism through which only students who really want to be a part would be given a pass.

  • How long is the event?

    The conference is a full day. It starts at 9:45 and ends at 14:30 hours.
